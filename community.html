How to get involved

  1. Pick a group or program that fits your interests (learning, mentoring, events).
  2. Sign up using the contact form or email hello@aurasense.org.
  3. Attend a workshop or request a one-on-one mentor session.
  4. Share your feedback so we can keep the community safe and welcoming.

Result: Users feel supported, motivated, and connected.
