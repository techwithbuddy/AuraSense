Making the world accessible for people with visual impairments

AuraSense provides resources, training, assistive-technology guidance, and community support for blind people and people with eye defects.

Keyboard shortcuts: Alt + R read, Alt + H toggle contrast, Alt + +/- font size

What we offer

  • Guides & Training

    Step-by-step tutorials for using screen readers, magnifiers, and accessible mobile features.

  • Assistive Tech

    Recommendations and reviews of accessible hardware and apps.

  • Community & Support

    Peer groups, volunteer programs, and local events.
